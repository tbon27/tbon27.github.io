Magic Slash

Inspired by a lesson on mouse properties in Unity using C#, this six-month solo 2D project evolved into a casual action-fantasy game for mobile devices. This exercise was designed to educate myself through experience in publishing a mobile game to Google Play Store, Samsung Galaxy Store. Magic Slash was released November 7, 2022 on the Samsung Galaxy Store and November 8, 2022 on the Google Play Store.
